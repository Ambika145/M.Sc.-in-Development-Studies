M.Sc. in Development Studies

A Postgraduate Programme by O.P. Jindal Global University (Office of Interdisciplinary Studies – IDEAS)

Transform Your Passion for Social Impact into Measurable Change.

Offered by JGU's Office of Interdisciplinary Studies (IDEAS), this forward-looking programme integrates policy, economics, and social research to equip professionals and graduates with the tools to lead transformative development initiatives.

Rooted in the belief that inclusive progress is both a science and an art, the M.Sc. in Development Studies prepares you to bridge the gap between research and real-world action — empowering you to design equitable policies, strengthen communities, and drive sustainable growth across global and local contexts.
